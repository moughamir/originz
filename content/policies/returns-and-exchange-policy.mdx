# Returns and Exchange Policy
_Last updated: October 5, 2025_

## Exchanges
We accept exchanges within **7 days** of receiving your order. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.

- **Exchange shipping costs:** Paid by the customer.
- **Return shipping costs:** Covered by Jhuangnyc.

To request an exchange, please email **support@jhuangnyc.com** with your order details.

## Process
1. Contact support to initiate your exchange.
2. Receive confirmation and shipping instructions.
3. Ship your item back as directed.

We will process your exchange once we receive your returned item.
